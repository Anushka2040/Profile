About Me



I am a recent graduate of Medi-Caps University in India, holding a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Throughout my academic journey, I've immersed myself in the study of complex algorithms and technology, culminating in the completion of four major projects focused on Python programming. These projects ranged from stock visualization and forecasting to developing a plant disease detection application using Convolutional Neural Networks.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I've gained practical experience through three unique internships. At The Bored Game Company, I excelled in web development and design, implementing innovative solutions to enhance user experience. My internship at Telexa Technology fueled my passion for applying technology to real-world challenges, particularly in the agricultural sector. During my final internship, I led projects focused on optimizing system performance and designing RESTful APIs.

Looking ahead, I am eager to further my knowledge in technology, with a focus on innovative solutions and transformative advancements. Beyond my technical interests, I am also passionate about art and community engagement, which have enriched my perspective and honed my communication skills.

As I embark on my career journey, I aspire to leverage my skills and experiences to make meaningful contributions to the field of computer science, ultimately bridging creativity and technology for impactful innovation.

About Me



With a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science Engineering from Medi-Caps University and a stellar CGPA of 9.12, I bring a solid foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to the table. Proficient in an array of programming languages including C/C++, Python, and JavaScript, as well as technologies like Node.js, Express.js, and TypeScript, I have honed my expertise through hands-on experiences.

During my tenure as a Software Development Intern at Gyizer Systems Pvt. Ltd., I led impactful projects such as developing an operating system kernel that significantly improved boot time, enhancing user productivity. Additionally, my role involved spearheading the creation of a subscription service, resulting in a substantial boost in customer retention.

Moreover, my contributions as a Frontend Intern at The Bored Game Company, where I utilized tools like Pelican, Jinja, and Figma to optimize website performance, underscore my versatility and proficiency in various technologies.

With a keen eye for innovation and a passion for problem-solving, I have also undertaken projects such as the development of dynamic web applications for stock visualization and weather forecasting, showcasing my ability to leverage cutting-edge technologies to address real-world challenges.

Complemented by relevant coursework in data science and web development, I am poised to make meaningful contributions and thrive in dynamic, collaborative environments.
